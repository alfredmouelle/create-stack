@alfredmouelle/create-stack

Compose your stack. Ship a real app.

An agnostic bootstrapper for your favorite framework. create-stack forks a fully-wired base app and strips it to exactly what you pick: database, auth, tRPC, a mailer, capabilities. Next.js and TanStack Start today, more frameworks on the way. The output builds and boots on day one. No template guesswork.

Node ≥ 22 · pnpm / npm / yarn / bun · MIT · open source
